Tiny Llama + AI Home Server

The Tiny Llama service is an open-source platform that offers completely free access to advanced AI technologies such as image diffusion and large language models. It prioritizes privacy by keeping data on the user's device and aims to accelerate AI adoption by making it accessible and affordable.

Open WebUI

Open WebUI is an extensible, feature-rich, and user-friendly self-hosted WebUI designed to operate entirely offline. It supports various LLM runners, including Ollama and OpenAI-compatible APIs.

SwarmUI

SwarmUI is a modular Stable Diffusion WebUI, with an emphasis on making image generation powertools easily accessible, highly performant, and extensible.
